Other Events Offered By Mental Health

  • Every first Saturday of the month at 7 pm in the Anoka County Community Center, we host an educational hour on a variety of topics such as identifying depression in a loved one, or how to start building your self-esteem.
  • Every other Sunday at 2 pm we also host a parenting group where new parents can learn about their child's development, and are provided tools for success in variety of fun, and educational topics. New Horizon day care center has donated an hour of their time to watch your little ones during this time.
  • Youth outreach is another avenue that Mental Health takes pride in achieving. Twice a year at local schools Mental Health professionals host a table where aside from handing out goodies; they provide information about disorders and self-help mechanisms such as reaching out if they need help, and coping skills.
