About

The Mental Health clinic aims to provide each patient inclusive, individualized care they truly need. Mental Health values respect for each individual, ethical care that honors both scientific understanding with empathetic practices, and attentive collaboration in the hopes of optimal treatment outcomes. Our vision is to transform the way psychological health is cared for, communicated, and practiced.

The Mental Health clinic is a small to medium sized mental health facility. All of our staff are highly trained and licensed. Each and every employee must pass a series of tests to ensure the quality of assistance they will be able to provide to our patience.

Our goal is to make sure that every single one of our patience feels valued and taken care of. We pride ourselves on having a clean, happy, and inviting atmosphere. We want every patient that walks into our facility to feel welcome.
