Services

Mental Health strives to reach every audience, so we offer a variety of services and outreach programs. From clinical counseling with our diversely trained psychologists and therapists, to free, fun, educational sessions in our local community center that cover a variety of topics.

Counseling Services- This facility has 22 diversely trained licensed therapists and psychologists ready to assist you or your loved ones. All of our practitioners are trained to treat concerns such as adjustment disorders, depression, and anxiety. We also have psychologists who specialize in post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), postpartum depression (PPD), complicated grief, eating disorders, substance abuse, and couples or family counseling.

Substance abuse- This facility provides a 8 to 12-week intensive outpatient program (OTP) that provides the needed services and support such as a 12-step program. This OTP still allows individuals to live life and carry out responsibilities such as going to work or school.

Group counseling- While it may be easy to numb out our feelings, or isolate when suffering from an invisible illness, this tends to produce feelings of being alone in life. Hearing a "me too." from a peer can beextremely supportive and facilitate healing. There is strength in numbers, monthly or weekly group therapy sessions are covered on a variety of topics and age groups from adolescent (12-17) to adult.

Outreach and Community- Mental Health mission is to provide mental health services to all, and we offer a variety of outreach services to the community. Click here to find out more.
